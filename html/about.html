About

Me

I’m an aspiring developer and designer, exploring a brand new world with coding.

Having myself experienced the lack of focus on user-experience and user-friendly websites/apps, I want my work to show that shiny and sparkles isn’t always the way to go. Simplified and beautiful design, clear visuals and subtle guidelines that explains a layout at a glance, and a non-stressful and non-disruptive use of call to actions are the three main goals I keep to.

WORK WISDOM

Being late at finding this path, I have experience in customer service, herbal remedies, office admin work, luxury tourism and even refinancing loans. Even though I’m what you would call a Jack of all trades, I appreciate each and every path I’ve walked earlier in life, as they have taught me valuable bits and pieces I wish to include in my current journey.
